Meet Me

Sunrises are unbeatable. They bring a feeling of fresh hope that makes almost anything feel possible. The world is crisp, the colors are vivid, and the day is fully new.

I would call myself an explorer. Since I was young, I have had a deep excitement about following unknown pursuits. Mystery and adventure stories were always my favorite, and I loved trying new hobbies. Still today I am always excited to learn a new skill, visit an unfamiliar place, or meet a stranger. I constantly find myself jumping into new projects or experiences and trying to learn about new ideas at every chance.

It can feel dim in the new space of the unknown. Everything is fuzzy, dark, and unclear. It can feel frustrating and intimidating. But then the sunrise breaks through and covers everything in light. The colors bring fresh energy, clarity, and hope. Once in the middle of the brightness, the unknown is intriguing and beautiful. With the thought of the sunrise in mind, there are so many places to go, people to meet, and things to learn.

Can't wait to see what's next!
